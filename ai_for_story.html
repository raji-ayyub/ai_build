CREATION ROADMAP

This curriculum is designed to help the learner become confident using AI for modern creative work: generating ideas, writing scripts and captions, creating promotional visuals, producing short-form videos, and packaging content for sharing. The goal is not only creativity, but practical fluency with the kinds of tools used in marketing and digital media today.

01 // CURRICULUM MODULES

A practical path into AI-driven
content and promotional media

Content Strategy and Messaging

Learn how to shape ideas into hooks, captions, short scripts, storyboards, and content plans. This module builds the thinking behind strong content before jumping into tools.
Output: 1 content plan with captions, hooks, and scripted concepts

AI Visual Design

Use AI image tools and design platforms to create branded visuals, thumbnails, posters, scene graphics, and content assets that look polished and consistent.
Output: visual asset pack for a mini campaign

Short-Form Video Production

Turn ideas and visuals into reels, promos, and short AI-assisted videos using voiceover, captions, music, timing, and editing techniques suitable for online promotion.
Output: 30 to 60 second promotional video

Branding, Publishing, and Growth

Learn how to package content for real audiences with thumbnails, descriptions, posting strategy, feedback, and simple performance review.
Output: finished content set ready for sharing or portfolio use

AI Prompting for Creative Work

Build confidence writing prompts for copy, visuals, voice, and editing support so the learner directs AI rather than passively accepting whatever it gives.
Output: reusable prompt bank for content creation

Portfolio and Presentation

Collect finished work into a small portfolio that demonstrates creative growth, digital confidence, and practical AI literacy in a way a parent can clearly see.
Output: mini showcase of the learner's completed work

04 // LEARNING FLOW

How the roadmap moves from
idea to finished content

1

Start with a Clear Content Goal

Each project begins with a simple goal: inform, promote, entertain, or present an idea clearly.

purpose first
2

Build the Message

The learner develops hooks, captions, short scripts, or scene plans before visual production begins.

messaging
3

Create Visual Assets

AI image tools and design platforms are used to generate visuals that match the message and tone.

visual creation
4

Add Voice and Audio

When appropriate, voiceover and music are added to strengthen the emotional and promotional effect of the content.

audio layer
5

Assemble the Video or Post

Content is edited into a complete piece with timing, captions, transitions, and final layout decisions.

production
6

Review and Refine

The learner checks quality, improves clarity, and makes revisions rather than stopping at the first output.

refinement
7

Share and Reflect

The final step is publishing, presenting, or archiving the work as part of a growing creative portfolio.

outcome
